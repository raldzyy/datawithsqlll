Classic Potato Salad Recipe

Ingredients

  • 5 pounds potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 3 hard-boiled eggs, chopped
  • 1 red onion, diced
  • 1 celery stalk, diced
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil potatoes in salted water until tender. Drain and cool slightly.
  2. In a large bowl, combine potatoes, eggs, onion, and celery.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk together mayonnaise, sour cream, Dijon mustard, and dill.
  4. Pour the dressing over the potato mixture and fold gently to combine.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve chilled or at room temperature.
